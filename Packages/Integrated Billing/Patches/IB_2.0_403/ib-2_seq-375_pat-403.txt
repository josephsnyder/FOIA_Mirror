Subject: E-CLAIMS - DIAGNOSIS POA INDICATORS

Category: 
  - Routine
  - Data Dictionary
  - Other

Description:
============

 This patch enables editing, transmitting and printing of Present On
 Admission (POA) indicators for each diagnosis on the given Integrated
 Billing (IB) claim.
  
 This patch also includes the following bug fixes:
  
 1. Fixed typo "N0W" to "NOW" (zero changed to letter "O") in routine
    ^IBCESRV2.
  
 2. Modified processing logic in ^IBCE277 to ensure that 277STAT
    messages are filed properly even if the confirmation message from 
    FSC was never received.
