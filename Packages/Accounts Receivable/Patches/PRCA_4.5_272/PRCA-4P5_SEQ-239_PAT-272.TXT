Subject: ARDC OIG GL NUMBER CHANGES

Category: 
  - Routine

Description:
============

 Some bills in the Office of the Inspector General (OIG) extract of the
 Accounts Receivable Data Collector (ARDC) are reported in the wrong
 General Ledger (GL) number and need to be corrected.  This patch makes 
 corrections to the GL accounts for reimbursable health insurance bills in 
 the OIG extract of the AR Data Collector.

 Tampa VAMC

 Remedy Overview:
 ================
 HD0000000401124 - MISCLASSIFICATION OF GENERAL LEDGER ACCOUNT
  
 Problem:
 --------
 GL accounts are incorrect for current bills in the OIG extract of the AR
 Data Collector.  They are currently being classified as GL account 1315 or
 1333.
  
 SV Type        G/L Number
 -------        ----------
  21              1315
  default         1333
  
 Resolution:
 -----------
 Routine RCRJRCOC has been modified to classify the current bills 
 into the correct GL account per specifications received from 
 the Veterans Health Administration (VHA) Office of the Chief 
 Financial Officer(CFO). The correct GL account classifications
 are as follows:
  
 SV Type        G/L Number     Description
 -------        ----------     -----------
  2F              13H1         Post-MRA Medicare bills
  2L              13N1         Post-MRA non-Medicare bills
  2A              1333         Tort Feasor
  21              1311         other current bills
  default         1311
